About Me

I am a third year undergraduate at Northwestern University with concentrations in dance, computer science, and cognitive science. I recently returned from Paris, where I developed professional proficiency in French and while interning at the Centre International de Danse Rick Odums, a professional jazz dance company and school.

My main interests include artificial intelligence and human-computer interaction, with the eventual goal of designing smart systems that adapt to people and organizations, allowing technology to become more accessible to everyone.

I am passionate about art, creativity, communication, and technology. My studies at Northwestern have allowed me to discover many connections between my varied interests, giving me the opportunity to explore these connections both independently and with guidance from peers and faculty members. In my spare time, I can be found in a dance studio training and choreographing with Graffiti Dancers and Refresh Dance Crew or managing publicity for Northwestern's New Movement Project.
