About Me

I am a senior undergraduate in computer science at Northwestern University. My main interests revolve around human-computer interaction and designing accessible technology.

I recently joined the Design, Technology, and Research team in the Delta Lab, where I am advised by Haoqi Zhang and Nell O'Rourke. Outside of my coursework, I work as an undergraduate TA for various introductory CS courses in C# and Racket.

In my spare time, I can be found in a dance studio training and choreographing for my secondary major in dance or working as artistic director for Graffiti Dancers, Northwestern's oldest student-run dance company.
