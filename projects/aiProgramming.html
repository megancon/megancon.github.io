Artificial Intelligence Programming

Northwestern University

Series of projects that my team developed for an artificial intelligence course under the instruction of Professor Sara Sood.

Skills

Python Programming, Artificial Intelligence Algorithms

Mancala Bot

We implemented the alpha-beta pruning search algorithm and developed a game board evaluator to create efficient and artificially intelligent game players.

Sudoku Bot

In order to solve sudoku puzzles of varying sizes and difficulties, we wrote a constraint satisfaction problem solver that utilized back and forward checking and other heuristics (minimum remaining values, degree, least constraining value).

Sentiment Analysis

My team developed a Naïve Bayes Classifier that analyzed the sentiment expressed in text. The classifier was trained and tested on a corpus of movie reviews. When a short selection of text was inputted, it returned whether the selection was positive, negative, or neutral.

Sketch Recognition

For this project, we applied our knowledge of Hidden Markov Models to develop a system that could distinguish between text and graphics in a sketch. We used the Viterbi Algorithm to determine the most likely sequence of labels (drawing or text) based on stroke length, stroke speed, and stroke curvature from a series of pen strokes. To calculate the accuracy of our classifier, we created a confusion matrix to calculate and compare how different strokes were classified, given their true labels.
